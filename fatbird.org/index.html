Info

Fatbird is a creative brand centered around a character-driven universe that explores storytelling through books and visual media. Built for a broad audience, it combines narrative fiction with a distinctive artistic identity, featuring original fanart and image-based content that expands its world beyond the written page. The brand emphasizes accessible storytelling for everyone, blending professional design standards with imaginative themes to create a cohesive and engaging experience across its book series and visual works. Fatbird’s identity is strengthened through its evolving collection of artwork, which helps bring its characters and stories to life in a recognizable and unified style.
